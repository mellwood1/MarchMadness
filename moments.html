Legendary March Madness Moments

Kris Jenkins Game Winner

In 2016, Villanova's Kris Jenkins hit a game-winning three-pointer at the buzzer to defeat North Carolina in one of the greatest championship games ever.

Saint Peter's Cinderella Run

In 2022, Saint Peter's became the first 15-seed ever to reach the Elite Eight, shocking basketball fans across the country.

UMBC Upsets Virginia

In 2018, UMBC became the first 16-seed to defeat a 1-seed when they upset Virginia.
