How the NCAA Tournament Works

The NCAA Tournament includes 68 college basketball teams from across the country. Teams are selected based on their performance during the season.

The tournament uses a single-elimination format. This means that if a team loses one game, they are eliminated from the tournament.

Tournament Structure

  • First Four
  • Round of 64
  • Round of 32
  • Sweet 16
  • Elite Eight
  • Final Four
  • National Championship

Teams are seeded from 1 to 16 in each region. Higher seeds are expected to perform better, but upsets happen every year.
